Kash Cube — Privacy Policy

Version 1.1 · Last Updated: March 22, 2026

OUR COMMITMENT
Kash Cube is built with privacy at its core. Your financial data is yours and yours alone.

DATA STORAGE

All data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. There is no cloud sync, no remote servers, and no login required. Your data never leaves your device.

What we store:

  • Transaction amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates
  • Party names and notes (stored locally)
  • Account and credit records

Where it is stored:

  • Android: App-private local storage
  • iOS: App sandbox (Application Support)

DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

  • ✗ Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • ✗ Location data
  • ✗ Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • ✗ Crash reports
  • ✗ IP addresses
  • ✗ Any financial data sent to third parties

ANALYTICS (opt-in, off by default)

Optional anonymous analytics via Firebase Analytics — OFF by default. Enable in Settings → Privacy → Anonymous Analytics.

When opted in, we collect:

  • Screen navigation events (e.g. "opened Reports")
  • Feature interaction events (e.g. "exported CSV")
  • App session metadata from Firebase (OS version, country)

We NEVER collect (even when opted in):

  • ✗ Transaction amounts, descriptions, or dates
  • ✗ Party names, balances, or contact details
  • ✗ SMS content
  • ✗ Any personally identifiable financial information

Disabling analytics stops all event collection immediately.

PERMISSIONS

Core app storage: Local SQLite files on your device

Declared on Android build:

  • Internet + network state (LAN web companion/P2P sync and optional analytics transport)
  • Wi-Fi/multicast access (LAN discovery and local connectivity)
  • Notifications + boot receiver + vibration (local reminders)
  • Camera (QR scan and camera capture flows)
  • Contacts read/write (optional import/save contact flows)
  • Biometric/fingerprint (optional app lock)
  • Billing/install-referrer permissions (Play Billing and install attribution)
  • Additional platform/SDK-injected permissions used by enabled dependencies

Runtime prompts shown only when feature is used:

  • Notifications (when enabling reminders)
  • SMS (when enabling SMS auto-detect)
  • Camera (when using QR/camera flows)
  • Contacts (when importing/saving contacts)
  • Biometric (when enabling biometric app lock)

DATA SHARING

We do not share any data — because we do not have access to it. Your data never leaves your device, is never uploaded, and is never sold.

SECURITY

  • Device-level security (lock screen, encryption)
  • PIN protected with PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 (100,000 iterations)
  • No data transmission = no network interception risk

YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access: View all your data in the app at any time
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to clear all data
  • Export: PDF/CSV export from within the app
  • Modify: Edit or delete any record

COMPLIANCE

  • GDPR: Compliant by design — no data collection
  • CCPA: Compliant — no data sale
  • COPPA: Compliant — no collection from anyone
  • DPDP Act 2023: Compliant

CONTACT

For support or privacy-related questions, contact us through the Play Store listing or email support.
